Quick Tips

  • Use thin layers of product — build coverage slowly.
  • Match foundation to jawline, not wrist.
  • Always clean brushes every 1–2 weeks to avoid breakouts.
  • Use a damp sponge for dewy finish; a brush for fuller coverage.
  • Practice different eyeliner styles on paper first (steady hand!).

Tools Checklist

  • Gentle cleanser & moisturizer
  • Primer & foundation
  • Concealer & setting powder
  • Neutral eyeshadow palette
  • Blending brush, flat shader, angled liner brush
  • Mascara, brow pencil, and a soft lip color

FAQ

Q: How long will I need to practice?
