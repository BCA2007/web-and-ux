Nutrition Basics

Fuel your progress: eat enough calories, focus on protein, and prioritize sleep and hydration.

Protein Targets

Aim for ~1.6–2.0 g protein/kg bodyweight daily for muscle growth (beginner guideline).

Simple Meal Ideas

Eggs + oats, Chicken + rice + veg, Beans + quinoa, Greek yogurt + fruit.

Form & Safety Tips

  • Always warm up 5–10 minutes (light cardio + mobility).
  • Prioritize form over heavy weights — controlled reps.
  • Rest 48 hours between working the same muscle group hard.
  • Get 7–9 hours sleep to support recovery and growth.
  • If unsure, consult a qualified coach or trainer.

Tools & Gear

  • Pair of dumbbells or resistance bands
  • Comfortable sneakers and water bottle
  • Workout log (notebook or app)

FAQ

Q: How fast will I see changes?
A: Visible changes usually take several weeks; strength improvements can show in the first 2–6 weeks with consistent training.

Quick Mobility
